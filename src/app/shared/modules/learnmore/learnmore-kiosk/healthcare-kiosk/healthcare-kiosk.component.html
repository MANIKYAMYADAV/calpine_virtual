KIOSK

Jaldee Kiosk feature allows you to place a Tablet/PC/Laptop/Other Machines in your premise to give your patients an easier and more interactive way to check-in themselves for the services offered by your enterprise.

How it works?

The KIOSK feature is designed to allow the walk-in patients to self check-in for the services offered by your enterprise.

  1. Check-In Yourself

    2. Patient can walk-in to any open Kiosk. The kiosk will prompt the patient to enter the mobile number and first three letters of their first name or last name. This is to look up if the patient is already been registered with Jaldee.

    • If the patient is already a registered member, he/she can check-in online for any service offered.
    • If the patient is not a registered member, he/she can register with Jaldee inputting name and can proceed with check-in.
    • In check-in window first select the service you wish to avail from the drop down menu. If the provider has only one default service there will be no drop down menu.
    • Check-in window has a feature to add or change a family member whom you wish to do check-in for. Click “Add/Change Member” to add new member with first name and last name and also can choose member from the list of members added already.
    • If you need to communicate with the provider you can click “If you have a note, Click here(+)” and can write the message in the textbox.
    • With this information you are ready to proceed with online check-in by clicking “CONFIRM CHECK-IN”.
    • Push message will be sent to patient's mobile number.
    • Alike Check-in, Future Check-in can be done in the same way. The only difference is that you need to select the date of check-in here.

      • Note: Other than above patient cannot access any other information of your jaldee account.

  2. Checked-in Online Check Your Status/Report as Arrived
    • Patients who have checked-in online can search for their status inputting mobile number and name.
    • The window shows the status of their check-in along with the token number, persons ahead, estimated waiting time. If he/she has arrived they can mark their status to Arrived clicking “CONFIRM ARRIVAL”.
    • A patient can also get registered with Jaldee by themselves and can proceed with check-in.
    • Initial step is the search process. With mobile number and first name/last name of patient, search for this particular entry in the system.
    • If the patient does not exists, he/she can input the patient details and get registered with Jaldee.
  3. Do You Want to Sign Up?

    4. Patients can sign up through the KIOSK machine with this option. All what patient need to do is just input the mobile number, first name and last name, and they will get registered with JALDEE.
