PROVIDER DASHBOARD

The dashboard section lets the doctor manage and monitor the check-ins of a patient. Here a doctor can monitor the current check-ins (Today) and the future check-ins (Future). The patient's previous check-ins can be viewed in this section under the “History” tab. To view the details, click on the patient’s name. There are location and working hours to help the doctor monitor the details of check-ins in different windows.

  1. Today () - All the present day’s online and walk-in check-ins are listed here. The adjoined brackets display the number of check-ins.
  2. Future() - All the check-ins for future dates are listed here. The adjoined brackets display the number of future check-ins.
  3. History - All of the patient’s previous check-ins until a day before are listed here. To view the details click on the “date of check-in” or on the “name of the patient”. Actions can also be changed from here.

Location Selection

Here a doctor can select the exact location if multiple locations are available. The doctor can check the details of a particular check-in for a specific location.

Note: A doctor needs to select the location of service while he/she checks-in a “walk-in” patient.

CHECK-INS

  • If the business has multiple locations, select the location and the working hour from the dashboard.
  • Here the doctor can see a list of patients who have checked in (Online or Walk-in ).
  • In some cases, a “cloud symbol” is prefixed to a name, it means that the patient has checked in online.
  • To view the check-in details of a patient, doctor can click on the name of the patient. Check-in history, communication history, and history of private notes are listed here. In addition to that, the user can cancel a patient’s check-in and inform this through Send Message. A doctor can add a private note for a patient using Add private note.
  • For “Manual” wait time calculation, the doctor can edit individual check-ins wait time from the check-in details.
  • Each of the patient’s Name, Wait Time and Service is displayed here.
  • The three dots menu in the right side of check-in has the following options:
    1. Send Message : Instantly type and send a message to the patient here. These messages can be seen in communication history anytime.
    2. Cancel check-in :
      • To cancel a patient’s check-in, click on ”Cancel Check-In”.
      • Patients are notified about the cancellation of Check-in through push message/email.
      • When a doctor clicks on “Cancel Check-In” a pop-up window appears which shows the reasons for cancellation of the check-in. Use the checkboxes to select from the suggested reasons and then click “Save”.
      • The doctor can inform the patient about the cancellation. To do this, click on the Send Message button. A text box will appear with a default message for each reason, doctor can either send the default message or can edit and type a personalized message.
    3. Add private note : In case the doctor wants to keep a personal note about a particular patient, he/she can write and add a note here. This note can be seen anytime the patient checks-in again with the doctor under “Private Notes”.
    4. Change Status to Check-in : The option to check-in a patient appears only when the patient’s status is “Arrived”. The doctor can change the status back to “Check-in” using this option. This can be used in the following circumstances:
      • When a patient gets cancelled for some reason and wanted to check-in again.
      • When a patient’s check-in status has been mistakenly changed to Arrived status.

ACTIONS

The following are the main actions the doctor can perform:

  1. Arrived : This button is used when a patient arrives at the premise of the business location to avail the services. When a doctor clicks this button, an “Arrived” label gets adjoined to the name of the patient. In case of a walk-in or kiosk patient, the “Arrived” label is automatically adjoined as the patients are present at the premises. Service can now be started.
  2. Start : Click on “Start” when the service is started. This will move the patient’s entry to “Started” tab. This will lead to the activation of the other two buttons, namely, “Complete” and “Bill”. Click on these buttons as and when required.
  3. Complete :Click on the “Complete” tab after completion of the service. The check-in status of the patient changes to complete. This will move the patient’s entry to the “Completed” tab. The completed tab generates two actions, namely “Bill” & “Accept Payment”.
  4. Cancel :If the service gets cancelled for some reason, click on the “Cancel” tab. This will remove the patient’s check-in entry from the list.
  5. Bill :After the completion of the service, a bill gets generated. Click on the “Bill” tab to view the receipt. Here a doctor can add extra services or items in the bill. From the three dots menu, a doctor can also “Apply order discount”, “Apply coupon” & “Apply Jaldee coupon” to the bill. The bill can be settled either by JaldeePay or Pay by Cash or Others.
  6. Accept Payment :Click on “Accept Payment”“Accept Payment” tab to receive the payment for the services. This opens the generated Bill page. Once the patient pays the bill, a doctor can settle the bill by clicking on the “Settle” tab.

STARTED

  • Under this tab, a doctor can view the list of patients who have already started availing the service. The check-in tab will no longer contain these patients entry.
  • Name, Wait Time and Service will be the same as that in the check-ins tab.
  • Two actions are available here:
  1. Complete : Click on the “Complete” button after the completion of the service. This will move the patient’s entry from Started tab to the Completed tab. In case the “End of Service Notification” button is enabled in the “Add Service”, a push message or email will be sent to the patient, thus informing them about the service completion.
  2. Bill : A “Bill” button appears when a service is started. This opens the bill generated for the service. Click on “Add Service/Item” in order to add more services or items to the bill before proceeding with the payment. The three dots menu near this button has the following options:
    • Apply Jaldee Coupon : These coupons have been provided by Jaldee.
    • Apply Coupon : These are coupons generated by doctor.
    • Apply Order Discount : These discounts are offered by the doctor.

      • *The option to Add Private Note and Patient Note is available here.

    • These coupons are applied to the total bill amount.
    • Discounts can either be applied to the Gross amount or to each item.
    • Three dots menu near service/item in the bill has the following actions:
      1. Change Quantity : Here a doctor can change the quantity of the item or service already added to the bill.
      2. Apply Discount : Here a doctor can apply a discount to the bill.
      3. Remove Service/Item : Click here to remove a service or item from the bill.
  • Additionally the option to “Add Private Note”, “Send Message”, and “Change Status to Check-in” is also available.
    1. Add Private Note : Write a note about a particular patient here.
    2. Send Message : Write text to the patient here and send it to the patient. All of the sent messages can be seen in communication history anytime.
    3. Change Status to check-in :The option to check-in a patient appears only if the patient’s status is marked to “Arrived”. A doctor can change the status back to “Check-in” using this option. This can be used in the following circumstances:
      • When a patient gets cancelled for some reason and wanted to check-in again.
      • When a patient’s check-in status has been mistakenly changed to “Arrived” status.
  • Payment Status: This shows the payment status (not paid, partially paid, or fully paid) of the patient.
  • Patient Note: When a patient adds any note while checking-in, it is displayed here.

COMPLETED

  • Once a service is done click on”Complete” button, the patient’s entry will move under the “Completed” tab.
  • The doctor is equipped with the following actions in this section:
    1. Accept Payment : Click on this button to view the saved bill. A doctor has the option to either continue with the payment procedure or to edit the bill by adding new services or items to the bill. The bill has the following options:
      • Settle Bill : Click on this button to settle the bill. The due amount in the bill is also visible in the patient dashboard.
      • Jaldee Pay : This enables the patient to pay online by generating the bill to the patient dashboard also.
      • Pay by cash or others : This enables the patient to complete the bill settlement either in cash or by other means.
  • Additionally the options “Add Private Note” and “Send Message” are also available.
    1. Add Private Note : Write a note about a particular patient here.
    2. Send Message : Write text to the patient here and send it to the patient. All of the sent messages can be seen in communication history anytime.

CANCELLED

  • Here the doctor can view the list of all cancelled patient check-ins.
  • The three dot menu available has the following options:
    • Add Private Note : Write a note about a particular patient here.
    • Send Message : Write text to the patient here and send it to the patient. All of the sent messages can be seen in communication history anytime.
    • Change Status to check-in : The option to check-in a patient appears only if the patient’s status is marked to “Arrived”. A doctor can change the status back to “Check-in” using this option.
