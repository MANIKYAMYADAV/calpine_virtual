PROVIDER DASHBOARD

The dashboard section lets the provider manage and monitor the check-ins of a customer. Here a provider can monitor the current check-ins (Today) and the future check-ins (Future). The customer's previous check-ins can be viewed in this section under the “History” tab. To view the details, click on the customer’s name. There are location and working hours to help the provider monitor the details of check-ins in different windows.

  1. Today () - All the present day’s online and walk-in check-ins are listed here. The adjoined brackets display the number of check-ins.
  2. Future() - All the check-ins for future dates are listed here. The adjoined brackets display the number of future check-ins.
  3. History - All of the customer’s previous check-ins until a day before are listed here. To view the details click on the “date of check-in” or on the “name of the customer.”. Actions can also be changed from here.

Location Selection

Here a provider can select the exact location if multiple locations are available. The provider can check the details of a particular check-in for a specific location.

Note: A provider needs to select the location of service while he/she checks-in a “walk-in” customer.

CHECK-INS

  • If the business has multiple working hours, select the working hour from the dashboard.
  • Here the provider can see a list of customers who have checked in (Online or Walk-in ).
  • In some cases, a “cloud symbol” is prefixed to a name, it means that the customer has checked in online.
  • To view the check-in details of a customer, provider can click on the name of the customer. Check-in history, communication history, and history of private notes are listed here. In addition to that, the user can cancel a customer’s check-in and inform this through Send Message. A provider can add a private note for a customer using Add private note.
  • For “Manual” wait time calculation, the provider can edit individual check-ins wait time from the check-in details.
  • Each of the customer’s Name, Wait Time and Service is displayed here.
  • The three dots menu in the right side of check-in has the following options:
    1. Send Message : Instantly type and send a message to the customer here. These messages can be seen in communication history anytime.
    2. Cancel check-in :
      • To cancel a customer’s check-in, click on “Cancel Check-In”.
      • customers are notified about the cancellation of Check-in through push message/email.
      • When a provider clicks on “Cancel Check-In” a pop-up window appears which shows the reasons for cancellation of the check-in. Use the checkboxes to select from the suggested reasons and then click “Save”.
      • The provider can inform the customer about the cancellation. To do this, click on the Send Message button. A text box will appear with a default message for each reason, provider can either send the default message or can edit and type a personalized message.
    3. Add private note : In case the provider wants to keep a personal note about a particular customer, he/she can write and add a note here. This note can be seen anytime the customer checks-in again with the provider under “Private Notes”.
    4. Change Status to Check-in :The option to check-in a customer appears only when the customer’s status is “Arrived”. The provider can change the status back to “Check-in” using this option. This can be used in the following circumstances:
      • When a customer gets cancelled for some reason and wanted to check-in again.
      • When a customer’s check-in status has been mistakenly changed to “Arrived” status.

Actions

The following are the main actions the provider can perform:

  1. Arrived : This button is used when a customer arrives at the premise of the business location to avail the services. When a provider clicks this button, an “Arrived” label gets adjoined to the name of the customer. In case of a walk-in or kiosk customer, the “Arrived” label is automatically adjoined as the customers are present at the premises. Service can now be started.
  2. Start : Click on “Start” when the service is started. This will move the customer’s entry to “Started” tab. This will lead to the activation of the other two buttons, namely, “Complete” and “Bill”. Click on these buttons as and when required.
  3. Complete :Click on the “Complete” tab after completion of the service. The check-in status of the customer changes to complete. This will move the customer’s entry to the “Completed” tab. The completed tab generates two actions, namely “Bill” & “Accept Payment”.
  4. Cancel :If the service gets cancelled for some reason, click on the “Cancel” tab. This will remove the customer’s check-in entry from the list.
  5. Bill :After the completion of the service, a bill gets generated. Click on the “Bill” tab to view the receipt. Here a provider can add extra services or items in the bill. From the three dots menu, a provider can also “Apply order discount”, “Apply coupon” & “Apply Jaldee coupon” to the bill. The bill can be settled either by JaldeePay or Pay by Cash or Others.
  6. Accept Payment :Click on “Accept Payment”“Accept Payment” tab to receive the payment for the services. This opens the generated Bill page. Once the customer pays the bill, a provider can settle the bill by clicking on the “Settle” tab.

STARTED

  • Under this tab, a provider can view the list of customers who have already started availing the service. The check-in tab will no longer contain these customers entry.
  • Name, Wait Time and Service will be the same as that in the check-ins tab.
  • Two actions are available here:
  1. Complete : Click on the “Complete” button after the completion of the service. This will move the customer’s entry from Started tab to the Completed tab. In case the “End of Service Notification” button is enabled in the “Add Service”, a push message or email will be sent to the customer, thus informing them about the service completion.
  2. Bill : A “Bill” button appears when a service is started. This opens the bill generated for the service. Click on “Add Service/Item” in order to add more services or items to the bill before proceeding with the payment. The three dots menu near this button has the following options:
    • Apply Jaldee Coupon : These coupons have been provided by Jaldee.
    • Apply Coupon : These are provider generated coupons.
    • Apply Order Discount : These discounts are offered by the provider.

      • *The option to Add Private Note and Patient Note is available here.

    • These coupons are applied to the total bill amount.
    • Discounts can either be applied to the Gross amount or to each item.
    • Three dots menu near service/item in the bill has the following actions:
      1. Change Quantity : Here a provider can change the quantity of the item/service already added to the bill.
      2. Apply Discount : Here a provider can apply a discount to the bill.
      3. Remove Service/Item : Click here to remove a service or item from the bill.
  • Additionally the option to “Add Private Note”, “Send Message”, and “Change Status to Check-in” is also available.
    1. Add Private Note : Write a note about a particular customer here.
    2. Send Message : Write text to the customer here and send it to the customer. All of the sent messages can be seen in communication history anytime.
    3. Change Status to check-in :The option to check-in a customer appears only if the customer’s status is marked to “Arrived”. A provider can change the status back to “Check-in” using this option. This can be used in the following circumstances:
      • When a customer gets cancelled for some reason and wanted to check-in again.
      • When a customer’s check-in status has been mistakenly changed to “Arrived” status.
  • Payment Status: This shows the payment status (not paid, partially paid, or fully paid) of the customer.
  • Customer Note: When a customer adds any note while checking-in, it is displayed here.

COMPLETED

  • Once a service is done click on”Complete” button, the customer’s entry will move under the “Completed” tab.
  • The provider is equipped with the following actions in this section:
    1. Accept Payment : Click on this button to view the saved bill. A provider has the option to either continue with the payment procedure or to edit the bill by adding new services or items to the bill. The bill has the following options:
      • Settle Bill : Click on this button to settle the bill. The due amount in the bill is also visible in the customer dashboard.
      • Jaldee Pay : This enables the customer to pay online by generating the bill to the customer dashboard also.
      • Pay by cash or others : This enables the customer to complete the bill settlement either in cash or by other means.
  • Additionally the options “Add Private Note” and “Send Message” are also available.
    1. Add Private Note : Write a note about a particular customer here.
    2. Send Message : Write text to the customer here and send it to the customer. All of the sent messages can be seen in communication history anytime.

CANCELLED

  • Here the provider can view the list of all cancelled customer check-ins.
  • The three dot menu available has the following options:
    • Add Private Note : Write a note about a particular customer here.
    • Send Message : Write text to the customer here and send it to the customer. All of the sent messages can be seen in communication history anytime.
    • Change Status to check-in : The option to check-in a customer appears only if the customer’s status is marked to “Arrived”. A provider can change the status back to Check-in”“ using this option.
