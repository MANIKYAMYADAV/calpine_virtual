Create Customer

  • A provider can create a customer profile.
  • The first step is that of the Search Process. To search for a customer in the Jaldee database, enter the customer’s mobile number and the first 3 letters of the customer’s first name/last name.
  • In case the customer’s information does not exist in the Jaldee database, the provider can input the customer details and get him/her registered with Jaldee.
