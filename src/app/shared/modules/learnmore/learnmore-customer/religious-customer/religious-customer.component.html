Create Devotee

  • A priest can create a devotee profile.
  • The first step is that of the Search Process. To search for a devotee in the Jaldee database, enter the devotee’s mobile number and the first 3 letters of the devotee’s first name/last name.
  • In case the devotee’s information does not exist in the Jaldee database, the priest can input the devotee details and get him/her registered with Jaldee.
