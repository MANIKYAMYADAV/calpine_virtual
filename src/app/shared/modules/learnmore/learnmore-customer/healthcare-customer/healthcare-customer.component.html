Create Patient

  • A doctor can create a patient profile.
  • The first step is that of the Search Process. To search for a patient in the Jaldee database, enter the patient’s mobile number and the first 3 letters of the patient’s first name/last name.
  • In case the patient’s information does not exist in the Jaldee database, the doctor can input the patient details and get him/her registered with Jaldee.
