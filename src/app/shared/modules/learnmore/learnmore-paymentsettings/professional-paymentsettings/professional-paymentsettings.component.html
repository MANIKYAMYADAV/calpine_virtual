Payments-Professional consultancy

In this section, you can select the payment mode you wish to accept or settle payments with customers.Payment Settings has two subsections, Online Payment Settings and Tax Settings.

  • To enable online payments using any payment system you have to switch “Accept Payments” to on mode. Initially this will be set to Off .
  • To enable “Accept Payments” button, you have to add an email id to your profile. From user profile you can add new email id.
  • If you have not entered an email id before, click the link “GO TO EMAIL SETTINGS” (Online Payment Settings) to add the email id. Verify the email id by entering the otp sent to the email. Once email verification is done, you can proceed to online payment settings.

    • Online Payment Settings

  • There are 2 modes of payment, that can be enabled.
    • Jaldee account : Jaldee account means, customers can pay online for the services to Jaldee account and in turn we the Jaldee team will reimburse the provider, the amount charging a commission for each transaction. For Basic license package, only Jaldee account is available for online payment. For higher license packages, providers can opt Jaldee account for online payment.
    • My own account : In this section you have to submit your account details of Paytm/CC/DC/Net Banking. Tick any mode of payment either “Paytm” or “CC/DC/Net Banking” or both.
      • Paytm : Paytm is an effortless mode of online payment commonly used these days. If provider has a Paytm account, provide the following:
        • Mobile Number
        • Merchant Id
        • Merchant Key
      • CC/Dc/Net Banking : Provide the following information for this mode of payment.
        • Account Holder Name
        • Bank Account Number
        • Pan Card Number
        • Bank Name
        • IFSC Code
        • Name on Pan Card
        • Branch Name
        • Business Filing Status
        • Account Type
  • To verify your bank account /Paytm account and to start accepting digital payments please call jaldee team on 0487-2325650 or mail us to support@jaldee.com.

    • Tax Settings

    Before setting up your taxes, make sure that you are familiar with the tax requirements for your business.The following instructions take you through the tax setup for your business services.

    • When you click tax settings under Payment section, a pop up window appears where you will see a textbox to enter the GST(Goods and Service Tax) number of your business and the tax percentage for the respective sector.
    • You should enter the GST number of your business first .
    • Next, Tax percentage should be entered .
    • When you have the right information click “Save”.
    • This tax will be applied to the services offered and items you are selling at the time of billing, only when you enable the “Tax Applicable” toggle button in respective sections(ie;Settings-->Waitlist Manager-->Services & Billing/POS-->Items).
    • We have no role in paying taxes paid by the customer to the service provider. All type of taxes are collected directly by the service provider, so it is the provider's responsibility to remit their taxes to the government.
