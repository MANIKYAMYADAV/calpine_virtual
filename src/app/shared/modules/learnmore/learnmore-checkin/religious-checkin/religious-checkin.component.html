Provider Dashboard You can monitor the current and future check-ins and also history of check-ins here.On the left side of the dashboard select the location and the queue available. Today () - Here the present day’s online, walk-in, and kiosk check-ins are listed.In the brackets appended to “Today” the total number of check-ins is displayed. Future() - Here the check-ins for future dates are listed. In the brackets appended to “Future” the total number of check-ins is displayed. History - Here you can see all those check-ins done till the day before. Present day’s check-in will be seen in the history only next day. You can also complete one devotee’s service from here. Location Selection If your enterprise has multiple locations that has been added to your JALDEE profile, select one location to proceed with processing the queue. Please ensure the location you selected, before you create or check-in a walk-in devotee. Check-ins Select the location, if your enterprise have more than one location. A detailed view of devotees who have already checked in online and also walk-in devotees created and checked-in at your desk is displayed. Prefix to sequence number if you see a “cloud” symbol, remember that particular devotee was checked in online themself. When you click on sequence number or on the devotee name, you can view complete check-in details of the devotee. Check-in history, Communication History and Private Notes history are listed here. In addition to that a provision to cancel devotee check-in ,communicate with devotee and add private note are available. The three dots menu in the right side of a particular check-in has the following options. Send Message : Write to your devotee from here. Cancel check-in : If you want to cancel a devotee check-in for any reason, you can click ”Cancel Check-In”. devotees are notified with push message/email, when their check-in is cancelled. When you click”Cancel Check-In”, a pop up window appears which shows few reasons listed with tick box .You can tick any valid reason from the list and click “Save”. If you want to inform your devotee about the delay caused,tick the Send Message button and a text box appears with a default message for particular reason. You can edit the message and can type your own message. Add private note : If you want to keep a note about a particular devotee, you can write the note here. This record will be seen anytime this devotee checks-in future. Change Status to Check-in : This option appears only if the status is marked to “Arrived”. You can change the status back to “Check-in” with this option. Following sequence number, Name and Service selected is displayed. Next focus is on “Actions” , where you have two actions “Arrived” and “Start” to perform. When the devotee who checked in online, arrives at the premise to avail his/her service you can mark “Arrived”. By that time “Arrived” label is appended to the name of the devotee. And also “ Bill” button comes adjacent to “Start” button with which new bill can be generated before getting service done. When the service starts click the button “Start” following which a message will be send to devotee informing the service has begun. Payment Status: Shows any payment status of the devotee, whether it is not paid, partially paid or fully paid. Devotee Note: The notes (if any) added by the customer while Check-in will be displayed here. Started Under this tab, the provider can view the list of devotees who have already started their service. Sequence number, Name and Service is the same as in check-ins tab. Mainly, two actions are performed here. Done: When the service is finished click “Done” button, and this devotee entry will proceed to “Done” tab. If you have enabled Notification button, either Push Message or Email in the “Add New Service” message will be sent to devotee informing the service has finished. New bill: When you click new bill a window with Name, date and time will be displayed. When you select the service devotee availed, details of price will appear there and can edit the standard rate if needed. When you have all the information click “Save”.// write more once it is completed In addition, Add private note ,Cancel check-in and Send Message options are also given. Add a provider note: If you want to keep a note about a particular devotee, you can write the note here. Cancel check-in: If you want to cancel a devotee check-in for any reason, you can click on Cancel check-in . devotees are notified with push message/email, if check-in is cancelled. When you click”Cancel Check-In”, a pop up window appears which shows few reasons listed with tick box. You can tick any valid reason from the list and click “Save”. If you want to inform your devotee about the delay caused,tick the Send Message button and a text box appears with a default message for particular reason. You can edit the message and can type your own message. Payment Status: Shows any payment status of the devotee, whether it is not paid, partially paid or fully paid. Devotee Note: The notes (if any) added by the customer while Check-in will be displayed here. Done Once the service is completed, that particular devotee entry comes under this tab. View bill and Accept Payments are the two important choices here.
