Billing/POS page is the one-stop-shop for adding items, discounts, coupons and tax settings. Discounts and coupons play a major role in driving customers toward a product/service and in boosting sales. Jaldee gives flexibility and ease of adding items and providing discounts or coupons to customers. This is only available from Platinum License package.

  • A provider can add/remove the item, service, discounts, and coupons anytime in the bill. The changes in the bill will be reflected immediately.
  • A provider can settle the bill only after the full amount is paid (either by paying the full amount, or applying discount/coupon/on demand discount).
  • Once settled a bill cannot be updated. The settled bill can be emailed.
Items

Under Items a provider can create and publish a list of items that are being sold at the premise. To add a new item, click “Add Item”. A new window appears where the provider can insert the information regarding the item such as its name, price, a short description, and a detailed description. If the item is taxable, the provider is required to enable the “Taxable” toggle button (the percentage of tax can be set in tax settings). This can be enabled only if the Tax settings is already set. Click “Submit” when all the information is filled. The provider can always change the quantity of the service or item in the bill. The changes will be reflected right away in the bill. This is only available from Platinum License package.

Discounts

To add a discount for the first time, click “Discounts”. Here the provider needs to enter the discount name, its type (Percentage/Fixed), and provide a description to the discount along with the value in Rupees. The discounts can be applied to the total bill as well as to each item and service. A provider can set up two types of Discounts:

  1. Fixed Amount: A specified amount will be deducted from either the total amount or from each specified item/service in the bill.
  2. Percentage: A specific percentage of the total amount from the invoice or from each item will be deducted in this case. “Percentage” type discounts can be applied either to the total amount on the invoice or to each specified item/service in the bill.

The discount dashboard shows all the existing discounts that have been created in Jaldee settings. Discount name, value, and description of each discount is visible here. A provider can create, delete and edit the discount. For creating more discounts, click on “(+)” symbol.

* Note: On Demand Discount is created during the account creation and its price is set to zero by default. It can only be applied to the total bill. It can be viewed under discounts list, right after the account creation. Its price cannot be updated. It can be used to waive the bill amount. At the time of bill updation, the provider can give any amount of on-demand discount by selecting this discount.

Coupons

This section is divided into two main sections, viz. Jaldee Coupons and My Coupons. Coupons, either Jaldee coupons or My Coupons can only be applied to the total bill amount.

  1. Jaldee Coupons :
    • The coupons provided by Jaldee are listed here. Each coupon has a code, name, validity date (from and to), status. The option to enable/disable the coupon is also available in the three dots menu.
    • The coupons with no three dots menu are enabled by the Jaldee. The provider has no option to enable/disable the coupon.
    • To view the details of a particular coupon, click on the coupon code.
    • In the detailed view, the provider can see the valid dates, discount value or percentage, rules, and the coupon stats as well.

    Following are the rules for each coupon. “✅” or “❌” indicates whether the rules are applicable or not.

    1. Always Enabled : This coupon will always be enabled. A provider has no option over enabling or disabling these coupons.
    2. Default Enabled : This coupon is enabled by default, but its status can be changed by the provider.
    3. Online Check in Required : This coupon is available only for those customers who check-in online.
    4. Self Payment Required : This coupon is available only for those customers who pay online for the services.
    5. Combine with Other Coupons : This coupon can be used with other available coupons.
    6. First Check-in Only : This coupon is available only for those customers who checks-in for the first time on Jaldee.
    7. First Check-in Per Provider Only : This coupon is available only for those customers who check-in for the first time for a particular provider.
    8. Consumer Use Limit Per Provider : This refers to the number of limited customers on whom a provider can apply the coupon.
    9. Minimum Bill Amount : This refers to the minimum bill amount to which the coupon can be applied.
    10. Consumer Use Limit : This refers to the number of times a customer can apply the coupon.
    11. Provider Use Limit : This refers to the number of times a provider can apply the coupon.
    • Here a provider can view the terms and conditions as well as the provider description for the coupon.
    • Corresponding customer description is displayed to him/her at their dashboard.
    • Coupon status is visible in the bottom of the page. The provider has the option to enable/disable the coupon here (only if permitted).
    • In addition, a coupon stat section is given where the following is shown:
      1. Check ins Applied : This shows the number of times check-ins applied for the coupon.
      2. Total Amount Discounted : This shows the total amount waived to the customers by the provider using the coupon.
      3. Amount Reimbursed by Jaldee.com : This shows the amount given to the provider by Jaldee.
  2. My Coupons

    These coupons are created by providers. To add a coupon for the first time click “Add Coupon”. Here enter the name of the coupon, type of the coupon (Percentage/Fixed) and provide a brief description to the discount. A coupon can only be applied to the total bill. A provider can set up two types of Coupons:

    1. Fixed Amount: A specified amount will be deducted from the total bill.
    2. Percentage: A specific percentage of the amount from the total bill will be deducted in this case. “Percentage” type coupons can be applied to the total bill.

    The Coupons dashboard shows all the existing coupons that have been created on the profile. Here a provider can create, delete and edit a coupon. To create more coupons, click on the “(+)” symbol.

Tax Settings

Before setting up the taxes, ensure that one is familiar with the tax requirements of one’s business. The following instructions will help the provider through the tax setup of the business services in Jaldee:

  • When the provider clicks on the tax settings under the Billing/PoS section, a window appears. The provider is required to fill the textbox (Enter the GST number of the business and the tax percentage for the respective sector here).
  • At first, enter the GST number of the business.
  • Next, enter the Tax Percentage.
  • After the complete insertion of the details, click “Save”.
  • This tax will be applied to the services offered and the items that are being sold at the time of billing, only if the “Tax Applicable” toggle button is enabled in the respective section (i.e. Settings -> Check-in Manager ->Services).
  • Jaldee has no role in paying taxes paid by the customer to the service provider. All types of taxes are collected directly by the service provider, so it is the provider’s responsibility to remit the taxes to the Government.
