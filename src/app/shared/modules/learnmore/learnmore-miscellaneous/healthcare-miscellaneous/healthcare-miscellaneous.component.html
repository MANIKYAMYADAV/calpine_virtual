NON WORKING DAY

Non-Working Day allows the doctor to set up different Public holidays and other non-working days. Unless a holiday has been mentioned in this section, it will be assumed that the service is being provided during the working hours specified under the Working Hours. Here is how a doctor can update non-working days in Jaldee.

  1. Click “Add Non-Working Day” under Miscellaneous.
  2. Select the corresponding date of the holiday (use calendar).
  3. Enter the reason for the holiday.
  4. Next, enter the duration of non-working hours. Keep in mind that the start time cannot be a different time from the timing of the working hours.
  5. Before adding a non-working day, ensure to have no pending waitlists for the day’s working hours.
  6. Upon having all the information, click “Save” to add the holiday.
  7. Subsequently, to add more holidays, click “(+)”.
  8. The date, time, and reason for each non-working day are listed here.
  9. A provider can edit or delete a particular non-working day. To do so, click on the three dots menu given in the right corner.
