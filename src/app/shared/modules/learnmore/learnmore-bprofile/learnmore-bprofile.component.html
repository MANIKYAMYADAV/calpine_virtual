PROFILE AND SEARCH

Your user profile contains basic information about you, which helps your consumers to search you from their dashboard. In your own profile you can access different features and also update information about yourself and the services provided by you.

PUBLIC SEARCH

Once consumer run a search from their dashboard, the search results appears in their dashboard.

  • If you want to display your profile in consumer search results,you have to click “TURN ON” button to make public search on.
  • If you haven’t set Public Search to “On”,consumers cannot see your profile in their search results.
JALDEE VERIFIED LEVELS

Once consumer run a search from their dashboard, the search results appears in their dashboard.

  • After the provider has created their profile, they will then be eligible to have their profile get verified with Jaldee.com.
  • Being verified by Jaldee.com allows clients, customers to know that you’ve been through the verification process and successfully verified.
  • Verified profile in consumer search results carries a flag with the profile name.
  • The priority in search increases with higher verification levels.Initially the verification flag is set to none.
  • Contact superadmin to verify your Jaldee profile.
  • There are 3 verified levels in Jaldee.com, Basic, Basic Plus and Premium.Details of each of the verified levels are depicted in the table below.

PRIVACY SETTINGS

Getting to know and controlling your privacy settings(Phone number & Email address) is an important part of helping protect your information.Here the system allows you to add, edit or delete both phone number and email address.

Phone
  1. You can view the phone number used to create this account and the visibility is set to none by default.
  2. The 3 dots menu on the right corner has two options Edit and Delete.
  3. If you want to edit an entry click on “Edit” button.Change phone number ,phone label and also the visibility.
  4. Visibility can restrict to who can see your phone number:
    • a. Public
    • b. Consumers only
    • c. None
  6. If you want to delete a phone number click “Delete” from 3 dots menu on the right corner.
Email
  1. You can add, edit and delete email id here.
  2. The 3 dots menu on the right corner has two options Edit and Delete.
  3. If you want to edit an entry click on “Edit” button.Change email id, email label and also the visibility.
  4. Visibility can restrict to who can see your email id:
    • a. Public
    • b. Consumers only
    • c. None
  6. If you want to delete an email id click “Delete” from 3 dots menu on the right corner.
