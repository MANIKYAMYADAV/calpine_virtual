Your user profile contains basic information about you, which helps your consumers to search you from their dashboard. In your own profile you can access different features and also update information about yourself and the services provided by you.

Public Search

Once consumer run a search from their dashboard, the search results appears in their dashboard.

  1. If you want to display your profile in consumer search results,you have to click “TURN ON” button to make public search on.
  2. If you haven’t set Public Search to “On”,consumers cannot see your profile in their search results.
    • Patients can search your profile with any attributes listed under the Profile & Search section. That can include business name,business description,specializations, and most of the additional info attributes or anything.
    • During the profile activation process, provider can proceed with setting up the Profile Name & Summary, Location,Working hours and Public search status. Option is given to setup the profile later, in Profile & Search under Settings tab, if needed.
    • If he/she logs out immediately after setting account password, the account will not be active unless and until the provider fill in all the details and turn Public search “ON”. A default service is created at this point which can be edited in Services under Waitlist Manager.
    • If he/she logs out after setting up the location, a default service and service time window is created. Provider can edit both these from settings page.
    • In below sections there are two labels SEARCHABLE and VIEWABLE which is set either to “Yes” or “No”.
    • Searchable indicates, with these attributes patients can search for a provider.
    • Viewable indicates, patients can only view these attributes in providers profile.

Privacy Settings

Getting to know and controlling your privacy settings(Phone number & Email address) is an important part of helping protect your information. Here the system allows you to add, edit or delete both phone number and email address.

Phone
  1. You can view the phone number used to create this account and the visibility is set to none by default.
  2. The 3 dots menu on the right corner has two options Edit and Delete.
  3. If you want to edit an entry click on “Edit” button. Change phone number, phone label(Example : Mobile,Emergency, Main Line or others) and also the visibility.
  4. The phone number you give here is viewed in your profile’s detailed view in patient dashboard, so that patients can contact you.
  5. Visibility can restrict to who can see your phone number:
    • Public
    • My Patients only (Patients who checks-in for any service, not necessary that they should complete the service).
    • None
  6. If you want to delete a phone number click “Delete” from 3 dots menu on the right corner.
Email
  1. You can add, edit and delete email id here.
  2. The 3 dots menu on the right corner has two options Edit and Delete.
  3. If you want to edit an entry click on “Edit” button.Change email id, email label(Example : Office Email Id,Personal Email Id etc) and also the visibility.
  4. The email id you give here is viewed in your profile’s detailed view in patient dashboard, so that patients can contact you.
  5. Visibility can restrict to who can see your email id:
    • Public
    • My Patients only(Patients who checks-in for any service, not necessary that they should complete the service).
    • None
  6. If you want to delete a email id click “Delete” from 3 dots menu on the right corner.
  7. It is good provide email id, that general notifications, notifications on coupons, discounts etc will be informed via email.
