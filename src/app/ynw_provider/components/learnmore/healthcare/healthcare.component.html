PROFILE AND SEARCH SETTINGS
The profile & search settings offers an in-depth overview of a doctor's profile. This would help patients make informed decisions and choose a relevant doctor. The dashboard will display every new details updated by the doctor, keeping patients aware of all changes occurring in the service.

Public Search
  1. A doctor is required to click on the “TURN ON” button to enable the Public Search if he/she wants to be visible in the patient’s search results.
  2. If a doctor has disabled the Public Search, patients cannot see the doctor’s profile in their search results.
  3. Patients can search for a doctor with any attributes that are listed under the Profile and Search section. These attributes can include the business name, Jaldee verified levels, Jaldee-Keyword, Basic Information, Location, Location Amenities, Specialization, Languages Known, Additional Info.
  4. Even if a doctor turns Public Search to off, patients can check-in with the same doctor from their “Favourites” (if already added doctor to Favourites List) list or using custom url.
  5. During the initial profile setup, a doctor can proceed with setting the Profile Name & Summary, Location, Working Hours, Service and Public Search status. The doctor can setup or update these informations here.
    • If the doctor logs out of the website after setting the password, the account will not be active. To activate the account, the doctor has to completely fill basic details and turn on the Public Search. At this point, a default service gets created which can be edited in Services under the Check-In Manager.
    • If the doctor logs out of the website after setting up the location, a default service time window gets created. The doctor can edit this from here.

    Privacy Settings
    Getting to know and controlling your privacy settings (Phone number & Email address) is an important strategy to protect your information. Here the system allows the doctor to add, edit or delete both phone number and email address.
    Phone
    1. A doctor can view the phone number used to create a Jaldee account. By default, the visibility of the number is set to none.
    2. The three dots menu on the right corner has two options, namely, Edit and Delete.
    3. If a doctor wants to edit an entry, he/she can click on the “Edit” button. Here, the doctor can change the phone number, phone label (for example, mobile, emergency, mainline or others) and also the visibility of the number.
    4. The phone number is shown in the profile’s detailed view in patient’s dashboard. This helps the patients to contact doctor easily.
    5. The doctor has an option to restrict the visibility of the phone number. Two options to filter the visibility are:
      • Public(All can see) : When set to Public, everyone can see the doctor’s number.
      • Private(None can see) : When set to Private, only the doctor can view the phone number.
    6. A doctor can also delete a phone number. To do so, click “Delete” from the three dots menu on the right corner.
         Email
    1. A doctor can add, edit and delete the email address here.
    2. The 3 dots menu on the right corner has two options, viz. Edit and Delete.

    4. The email address provided here is shown to the patients in the detailed view of the doctor profile.
    5. The doctor has an option to restrict the visibility of the email address. Two options to filter the visibility are:
      • Public(All can see) : When set to Public, everyone can see the doctor’s email.
      • Private(None can see) : When set to Private, only the doctor can view the email.
    6. A doctor can delete an email address. To do so, click “Delete” from the three dots menu on the right corner.
    7. Jaldee.com uses the email address to communicate the general notifications to the doctor, such as notifications on invoice, Jaldee coupons, discounts, etc.

    CHECK-IN MANAGER
    The Check-in manager helps the doctor in managing the working hours and ensuring the seamless workflow from the point of the check-in to the completion of service.

    Accept Online Check-in
    • Here, a doctor gets full control over online patient check-in.
    • Complete the “Profile & Summary” section with details. To do so, click on the “Profile” under Settings and enter the details. Once done, set Public Search to “ON”.
    • Afterwards, a doctor can enable “Online Check-in”.
    • Doctor can choose two options:
      • Same Day Online Check-ins : Turn on this toggle button to accept check-ins for the same day.
      • Future Check-ins : Turn on this toggle button to accept check-ins for a future date.
    • Walk-in check-ins are always enabled.
    Calculation of waiting time
    Waiting time simply refers to the time a patient has to wait before he/she can avail the services offered at the premise.
    • Automatic (Machine Learning) : Using a machine learning algorithm, the system automatically calculates the turnaround time for each service by analyzing a doctor’s service history.
    • Manual : Using this option, a doctor can fix a wait time which will be applied to all the check-ins (for example, If a doctor sets 10 minutes of manual time, by default all the patients will get 10 minutes of waiting time.) Manual time should be used when a doctor can guarantee to serve all the patients in the given time. To do so, it is advisable that the doctor is equipped with enough resources. An additional option is available for doctors choosing “Manual” wait time calculation. He/She can edit individual check-ins wait time from the check-in details in doctor dashboard.
    • Token System(Do not show waiting time for patients) : If a doctor chooses this option, then all the patients will receive and see a token number instead of any waiting time.
    • No Token : If a doctor chooses this option, the patients won’t be able to see the waiting time as well as the token number. Here a patient can see the number of persons ahead of him/her.

    Working Hours
    Working Hours simply means the active time of the business. One can add multiple Working Hours according to the business schedule. To understand this with an example, suppose a business works from Monday to Friday (9 am to 5 pm) whereas, on Weekends (i.e. Saturday and Sunday), it operates only for 4 hours (9 am to 1 pm). In such cases, one can create two working hours, one for weekdays and the other one for the Weekends.
    • This page prompts to “Add working hours”, in case the doctor has not added any working hour during the initial profile set up.
    • “ADD WORKING HOURS” button enables the doctor to add new working hours. When a doctor clicks this button a window appears where one can input the details of the schedule.
      • Working Hours : Enter a title for the working hours here.
      • Location : Choose a location from the drop-down menu.
      • Service : All added services offered by the doctor are displayed here. Check the services offered in this working hour from the list of services displayed here.
      • Schedule : Select the days according to the schedule. A doctor has the option to choose “Select all days”, in case the business is open 7 days a week.
      • Start Time/End Time : Enter the start time and the end time for a particular working hour.
      • Maximum patients served : Enter the maximum number of patients the business can serve in particular schedule. No more check-ins will be accepted, once the limit is reached. If the limit is not set, then endless check-ins will be accepted between the working hours.
      • Patients served at a time : Here enter the number of patients that can be served at the same time.
      • Online check-ins : Enable this toggle button to make this particular working hours available for patients to check-in online for the same day.
      • Future check-ins : Enable this toggle button to make this particular working hours available for patients to check-in online for a future date.

         My Coupons
    These coupons are created by doctors. To add a coupon for the first time click “Add Coupon”. Here enter the name of the coupon, type of the coupon (Percentage/Fixed) and provide a brief description to the discount. A coupon can only be applied to the total bill. A doctor can set up two types of Coupons:
    1. Fixed Amount: A specified amount will be deducted from the total bill.
    2. Percentage: A specific percentage of the amount from the total bill will be deducted in this case. “Percentage” type coupons can be applied to the total bill.
    The Coupons dashboard shows all the existing coupons that have been created on the profile. Here a doctor can create, delete and edit a coupon. To create more coupons, click on the “(+)” symbol.
    Tax Settings
    Before setting up the taxes, ensure that one is familiar with the tax requirements of one’s business. The following instructions will help the doctor through the tax setup of the business services in Jaldee:
    • When the doctor clicks on the tax settings under the Billing/PoS section, a window appears. The doctor is required to fill the textbox (Enter the GST number of the business and the tax percentage for the respective sector here).
    • At first, enter the GST number of the business.
    • Next, enter the Tax Percentage.
    • After the complete insertion of the details, click “Save”.
    • This tax will be applied to the services offered and the items that are being sold at the time of billing, only if the “Tax Applicable” toggle button is enabled in the respective section (i.e. Settings -> Check-in Manager ->Services).
    • Jaldee has no role in paying taxes paid by the patient to the doctor. All types of taxes are collected directly by the doctor, so it is the doctor’s responsibility to remit the taxes to the Government.
    DOCTOR PROFILE
    The doctor profile is a collection of settings and information associated with a business owner. The system creates a doctor profile at the time of registration. The profile contains information such as name, mobile, email, etc. Jaldee doctor profile has the following options:
    • My Account : Here lies the personal data of the service doctor. Option to edit the data is also available.
    • Change Password : Here the doctor can change the password keeping in line with the following guidelines:
      • Minimum 8 characters
      • Maximum 32 characters
      • Must have at least one uppercase letter and one digit.
    • Change Mobile : Enter a new mobile number and click on Submit. To verify the mobile number, enter the OTP sent to the number.
    • Logout : One can log out of the account here.

      • Alerts : All the Jaldee Broadcasts are shown here. These are the Jaldee provided notifications. Here a doctor can learn about new coupons or any other such information directly from Jaldee Super admin.
      • Audit Log : Here the doctor can view the activities done by him/her. The details appear with a date and time stamp. It also shows the actions completed by the doctor along with a little description.
